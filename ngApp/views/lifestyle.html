Read, Travel, Track Goals - Stay Organized!

The Bucketlist Lifestyle is not about living your life one list item at a time, it’s a lifestyle for people who want more than the day to day lives they have been living. You want to live the life of your dreams and are willing to get creative to do it! This doesn’t have to mean jet-setting to Tahiti on the weekends after skiing in the swiss alps all week, it could mean sitting down and writing out a a blueprint for fun activities, travel plans, time with family. It could be anything and you can add to your Bullet Journal to help you plan! Enjoy!
