Stay Fit & Healthy

One of the most important things you’ll do on your journey to health and wellness is to track your fitness progress. Knowing you’re on the right track is essential for keeping motivation high. Unfortunately, the scale is not the best tool you can use, but it’s the one people most commonly rely on. Figuring out what works for YOU is really the key to being successful in anything. And that’s precisely why bullet journaling can be such a huge help!! Because it’s so flexible, you can make it be exactly what you need it to be. If something isn’t working, you can leave that page behind and try something new! So, that would be my first piece of advice: Don’t be afraid to experiment. If something isn’t working for you, stop it and try something else. Never be afraid to try new ideas!
